Explore LinkNYC Kiosks in New York City

LinkNYC is a city-wide network of free, high-speed public Wi-Fi kiosks in New York City.
These kiosks replace outdated payphones and provide free internet, phone calls, device charging, and access to city services.
There are two types of kiosks: LinkNYC Classic (Link1.0) and Link5G.
Link1.0 units offer Wi-Fi and essential services, while Link5G kiosks include upgraded hardware and 5G antennas to expand high-speed wireless coverage across the city.
